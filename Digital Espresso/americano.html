Americano


Digital Espresso's Americano is produced by blending freshly pulled espresso shots with hot water to create the size of a conventional cup of brewed coffee.
It is  a terrific beverage to add to your go-if to's if you want to class up your normal morning-drip but don't want to add a load of sweetener and milk!
