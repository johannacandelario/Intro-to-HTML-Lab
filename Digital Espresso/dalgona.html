Dalgona

Digital Espresso's Dalgona coffee is a creamy beverage produced by combining equal amounts of instant coffee powder, sugar, and hot water, then topping it off with cold or hot milk!
To better suit their tastes, our customers are also given the option to add a dash of coffee powder, chocolate, crumbled biscuits, or honey!
