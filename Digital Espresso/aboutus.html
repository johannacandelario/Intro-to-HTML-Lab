About Us



Digital Espresso will be the place to go for high-quality coffee. We intend to provide our customers only the greatest freshly made coffee in the world, as well as divine snacks, exceptional service, and a price that is fair to both its producers and customers in a homey, pleasant environment. The core of Digital Espresso is defined by our dedication to benchmarks in customer service, employee well-being, and community involvement.

Johanna Candelario, the owner, has over a decade of experience in the food service sector, including business management. She highlights her goal of making Digital Espresso the first-choice destination for anyone who wants to unwind, socialize, and enjoy the best coffee and food – not only establishing a steady repeat customer base, but also a welcoming, comforting home and family in every spot we serve.

Though there are other coffee businesses in the region, we feel there is a demand for more, particularly ones that emphasize on higher margin gourmet coffee products such as espressos, cappuccinos, and others alike.
